About Me

My name is Habby Olusesi. I was born and raised in Raleigh, NC. I am 21 years old. My birthday is December 6th 1998. I graduated from Wake Forest University this year, May 2020. While there, I majored in Interdisciplinary Mathematics and Theatre. I also had a minor in Film Studies. I am currently enrolled in a software development program through UNC. The program will give me skills in languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I graduate from the bootcamp in February.

I love to travel so I spent a descent amount of my time at Wake Forest studying abroad. I visited places such as England, Switzerland and Cuba. Some of my biggest passions are Theatre and Film - specifically writing, directing and acting. I also love music. At the moment, I am learning to play acoustic guitar. Musical theatre, where my passion for music and theatre meet, is also a big love of mine. My favorite shows include 'Rent', 'Once On This Island' and 'Falsettos'. Some of my favorite musical artists are Giveon, J. Cole and Kevin Garrett.
